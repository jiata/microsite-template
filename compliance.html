Azure Virtual Networks
Get started with Virtual Networks
Keep your networks secure from the public and other machines in the cloud using Virtual Networks. Each Virtual Network is isolated from other Virtual Networks so that your data remains private.
VPN + Express Route
Get started with Express Route
Enable a connection from your datacenters to Azure Virtual Networks using Site-to-Site and Point-to-Site VPNs. For even better performance, you can use ExpressRoute, a private connection that keeps your traffic off the Internet.
